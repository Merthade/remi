How It Works

Three ways to remember things

Not every task on your checklist needs a hard deadline. Remi lets you choose how specific you want to be.

Tuesday, March 10 at 9am

For birthdays, appointments, and deadlines that actually matter.

Approximate date

Early March. Mid-April. Late June.

For things that don't need a hard deadline.

Someday

No date at all.

For bucket list dreams and "I'll get to it eventually."

Say Hello

This is Remi.

A small rabbit in a green vest who lives inside your app. He changes what he's doing based on what you're doing, but his expression never changes. Deadpan. Supportive. Unbothered.

He holds a clipboard when you're checking reminders, naps in a hammock when things are quiet, and gets buried in papers when things pile up. He doesn't judge. He literally can't.
